About Muhammad Awais

Muhammad Awais is a highly qualified Mathematics specialist and Biomaths researcher with over 10 years of teaching experience. He has helped hundreds of students excel in Pakistani and international curricula including Cambridge O/A Levels, IGCSE, GCSE, CSS/PMS, ISSB, and Cadet College entrance exams.

As the founder of AFS Virtual Tutor Hub, his mission is simple — democratize quality education by connecting students with the best teachers, completely free of charge.

"I built this platform because I believe every student deserves access to quality education, regardless of their financial situation. As a teacher myself, I understand the challenges students face. AFS Virtual Tutor Hub is my contribution to the future of Pakistan."
— Muhammad Awais, CEO

Why I Built AFS Virtual Tutor Hub

After years of teaching, I noticed that many talented students couldn't afford quality tutoring. The AFS Virtual Tutor Hub was born from this gap — a completely free platform where students can browse verified teachers, request demo classes, and access free study materials without paying a single rupee to the platform.

Every teacher on this platform is personally reviewed and approved by me to ensure the highest quality of education for our students.
