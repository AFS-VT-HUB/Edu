Frequently Asked Questions — AFS Virtual Tutor Hub

Everything you need to know about AFS Virtual Tutor Hub — finding online tutors, requesting free demo classes, teacher registration, and connecting with verified teachers for Cambridge, IGCSE, CSS/PMS, ISSB and more.

Is AFS Virtual Tutor Hub free for students?

Yes — browsing teacher profiles, requesting demo classes, and accessing study materials is completely free for students. Teachers set their own tutoring rates directly with students. AFS Virtual Tutor Hub never charges students any platform fee.

What types of courses and educational streams are available?

We cover all major curricula — Pakistani Matric, FSc (Pre-Medical & Pre-Engineering), Cambridge O/A Levels, IGCSE, GCSE, Edexcel, CSS/PMS exam preparation, ISSB preparation, Cadet College entry tests, MDCAT Biomaths, and international boards including Indian, Gulf, and European curricula.

Do you offer physical home tuitions or only online classes?

Our platform is primarily online — all classes are conducted via WhatsApp video, Zoom, or Google Meet. However, some teachers also offer in-person home tuition depending on their location. You can check this in the teacher's profile or ask directly via WhatsApp.

Are there any custom special classes or crash courses available?

Yes. Many teachers on AFS Virtual Tutor Hub offer crash courses, mock paper practice, and intensive exam prep sessions before Cambridge, Matric, FSc, CSS/PMS, and ISSB exams. Contact any teacher directly via WhatsApp to ask about availability.

Does AFS Virtual Tutor Hub provide scholarships?

Yes. Deserving students who cannot afford tuition fees can reach out to CEO Muhammad Awais directly on WhatsApp (+92 312 0940518). We review cases individually and connect deserving students with teachers willing to offer free or discounted classes.

What is the minimum qualification required to register as a teacher?

Teachers must hold at least a Bachelor's degree from an accredited university. Experience teaching Cambridge, IGCSE, CSS/PMS, or Pakistani board subjects is preferred. All applications are personally reviewed by CEO Muhammad Awais before approval.

How does the teacher hiring and registration pipeline work?

Fill out the Teacher Registration form on our website, upload your qualifications and a demo teaching video. CEO Muhammad Awais personally reviews every application. Once approved, your profile goes live and students can contact you directly via WhatsApp.

Where is the physical operational base location of the platform?

AFS Virtual Tutor Hub is based in Peshawar, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. It operates as an online platform, serving students across Pakistan as well as Pakistani students in the UAE, Saudi Arabia, UK, and other countries.

How can I directly contact the CEO for escalations or partnerships?

You can reach CEO Muhammad Awais directly on WhatsApp at +92 312 0940518 or via email at afsvirtualtutorhub369@gmail.com. For partnerships, teacher registration queries, or any escalations — he personally responds to all messages.
