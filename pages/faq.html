Student & Course Information
01

Is AFS Virtual Tutor Hub free for students?

Yes — browsing teacher profiles, requesting demo classes, and accessing study materials is completely free for students. Teachers set their own tutoring rates directly with students. AFS Virtual Tutor Hub never charges students any platform fee.
02

What types of courses and educational streams are available?

We cover all major curricula — Pakistani Matric, FSc (Pre-Medical & Pre-Engineering), Cambridge O/A Levels, IGCSE, GCSE, Edexcel, CSS/PMS exam preparation, ISSB preparation, Cadet College entry tests, MDCAT Biomaths, and international boards including Indian, Gulf, and European curricula.
03

Can I get a free demo class before paying a teacher?

Yes. You can request a free demo class from any teacher on AFS Virtual Tutor Hub. Use the WhatsApp button on the teacher profile or fill the Demo Request form on the Faculty page. There is no obligation to continue after the demo.
04

Do you offer physical home tuitions or only online classes?

Our platform is primarily online — all classes are conducted via WhatsApp video, Zoom, or Google Meet. However, some teachers also offer in-person home tuition depending on their location. Check the teacher's profile or ask directly via WhatsApp.
05

Are there crash courses or special exam prep sessions available?

Yes. Many teachers offer crash courses, mock paper practice, and intensive exam prep sessions before Cambridge, Matric, FSc, CSS/PMS, and ISSB exams. Contact any teacher directly via WhatsApp to ask about availability and scheduling.
06

Does AFS Virtual Tutor Hub provide scholarships for deserving students?

Yes. Deserving students who cannot afford tuition fees can reach out to CEO Muhammad Awais directly on WhatsApp (+92 312 0940518). Cases are reviewed individually and deserving students are connected with teachers willing to offer free or discounted classes.
Teacher Registration

Are the teachers on AFS Virtual Tutor Hub verified?

Yes. Every teacher is manually reviewed and personally approved by CEO Muhammad Awais before their profile goes live. This ensures only qualified, experienced teachers are listed on the platform. Student reviews are also visible on each teacher's profile.

What is the minimum qualification required to register as a teacher?

Teachers must hold at least a Bachelor's degree from an accredited university. Experience teaching Cambridge, IGCSE, CSS/PMS, or Pakistani board subjects is preferred. All applications are personally reviewed by CEO Muhammad Awais before approval.

How does the teacher registration pipeline work?

Fill out the Teacher Registration form on our website, upload your qualifications and a demo teaching video. CEO Muhammad Awais personally reviews every application. Once approved, your profile goes live and students can contact you directly via WhatsApp — all at zero cost.
