Frequently Asked Questions

Find instant detailed answers to your questions regarding our admission fee structures, premium physical locations, courses tracking, hiring pipelines, and support.

What is the tuition fee structure on this platform?

Registration on the platform is completely free for both students and parents. The actual tuition fee varies because instructors set their own hourly rates based on their experience and curriculum. Hourly rates are transparently displayed on each teacher's workspace profile card.

What types of courses and educational streams are available?

We support comprehensive curriculum paths, including Cambridge O Level, A Level, IGCSE, local board Matriculation, F.Sc Pre-Medical/Pre-Engineering, and highly specialized professional training tracking tracks such as CSS and PMS preparation focus tracks.

Do you offer physical home tuitions or only online classes?

While our core delivery model utilizes an advanced online virtual streaming studio classroom setup, select certified premium instructors do offer specialized in-person home tuitions based on their geographical tracking zones and student requirements.

Are there any custom special classes or crash courses available?

Yes, our faculty regularly schedules specialized targeted exam preparation crash courses, mock evaluation paper checking runs, and intense special session tracks ahead of final board or Cambridge assessment windows.

Does AFS Virtual Tutor Hub provide scholarships?

Yes, under our social educational support framework, exceptional and deserving students scoring top ranks can apply for specialized platform scholarship slots to access standard tuition assistance files.

What is the minimum qualification required to register as a teacher?

To maintain our high premium teaching standards, instructors must hold at least a completed BS (Bachelor of Science) or equivalent degree from an accredited university. Prior experience teaching specialized target boards (Cambridge or local boards) is highly preferred.

How does the teacher hiring and registration pipeline work?

Interested educators can complete their online registration form, submit their digital portfolio/credentials, and upload a dedicated video lecture link. Once submitted, your tracking file moves into our evaluation pipeline for administrative review and document screening.

Where is the physical operational base location of the platform?

Our operational headquarters and educational deployment center are located on Charsadda Road, Peshawar, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. All digital streaming architectures and physical verification workflows are managed directly from this location.

How can I directly contact the CEO for escalations or partnerships?

For premium partnerships, institutional tie-ups, or executive escalations, you can request a formal digital appointment window. You can reach out directly via our corporate communications channel, or escalate an advisory ticket marked to the attention of CEO Muhammad Awais.
